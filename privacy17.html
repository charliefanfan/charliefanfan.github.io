Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

FitSpace (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (“FitSpace”) handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

FitSpace does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information such as your name or email address.

However, the app may use third-party services that may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device information
  • Approximate location (based on IP)
  • Advertising identifiers
  • App usage information

2. Data Stored on Your Device

FitSpace stores certain information locally on your device to provide app functionality. This information does not leave your device.

Examples include:

  • Workout history
  • Exercise preferences
  • Workout progress
  • App settings
  • Streaks and activity statistics

3. Advertising

FitSpace may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect and use information to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

For more information, please refer to Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

FitSpace may offer optional premium features through in-app purchases or subscriptions.

All payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to or store your payment information.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services including:

  • Apple App Store services
  • Apple in-app purchase system
  • Advertising networks such as Google AdMob

These third-party services may collect information used to identify you.

6. Analytics and Tracking

FitSpace does not include additional third-party analytics services. However, advertising networks may collect usage information to improve advertising performance.

7. Children's Privacy

FitSpace is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
