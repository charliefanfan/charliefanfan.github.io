CatGoalSaver Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

Introduction

The CatGoalSaver application (referred to as "the App") is committed to protecting your personal privacy. This policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

I. Information We Collect

To provide the core functionalities and optimize our services, the App may collect the following types of information:

Contact Information:

  • Email Address: Used for creating and managing your CatGoalSaver account, login verification, password resets, and sending important service notifications.

Financial Information:

  • Other Financial Information: Used to track savings goals, income, assets, liabilities, and transaction records you input into the App for calculating financial progress and reports.

Identifiers:

  • User Identifiers: Used to identify your account, store your personal data, and provide personalized service.
  • Device Identifiers: Used for App analytics, fraud prevention, and measuring the effectiveness of our internal marketing campaigns.

Purchases:

  • Purchase History: Used to verify your in-App purchase or subscription rights and track purchase trends to optimize the product.

Usage Data:

  • Product Interaction: Includes your clicks, scrolls, App launches, and usage frequency, used to understand how features are utilized.
  • Advertising Data: Used to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns and track the source of App downloads for attribution purposes.

Diagnostics:

  • Crash Data, Performance Data: Used to monitor the App's stability, launch speed, power consumption, and crash occurrences.

II. How We Use This Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To authenticate your identity, store and manage your financial goals, provide customer support, prevent fraud, implement security measures, and ensure server uptime.
  • Analytics: To evaluate user behavior, understand the effectiveness of product features, measure audience size, and help us plan new features.
  • Product Personalization: To customize the App interface, provide personalized financial tips and recommended content based on your savings progress, purchase history, or usage habits.
  • Developer's Advertising or Marketing: Used to send you communications related to the App (such as newsletters or new feature announcements) and to measure the effectiveness of our own advertising and marketing campaigns.
  • Other Purposes (Bug Fixing & Optimization): Used for product development, maintenance, optimization, and promptly fixing bugs to improve App performance.

III. Information Sharing

We commit not to sell or share your personal data, financial information, or purchase history with Third-Party Advertisers for advertising purposes. We will only disclose your information when required to comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms of service, or protect the rights, property, or safety of us or others.
