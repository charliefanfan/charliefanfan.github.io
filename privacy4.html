Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the GiftMatch: Gift Planner mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our App.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The App uses Firebase (provided by Google Inc.) to help us improve the user experience and maintain App stability. The following data may be collected automatically:

  • Identifiers: Anonymous device identifiers (such as IDFV) are used to distinguish unique users for basic analytics.
  • Usage Data: Information regarding App launches, button clicks, and page views is recorded to help us understand feature performance.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance data are collected to help the developer identify and fix software bugs.

2. Data Linking and Identity

We do not link the collected data to your personal identity. The App does not require user registration, and we do not collect your name, email address (unless you contact us directly), or phone number. Any personal information you enter within the App (such as gift recipient names or preferences) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData technology and is not uploaded to our servers.

3. Tracking and Third-Party Sharing

We do not use the collected data for tracking purposes. We do not share or sell your data to third-party advertisers or data brokers for cross-app advertising. All data is used strictly for internal analytics and app functionality improvements.

4. Your Rights

Since the App stores your gift planning data locally, you can delete all such data at any time by simply deleting the App from your device. For the anonymous diagnostic data collected by Firebase, you may opt-out by discontinuing the use of the App.
