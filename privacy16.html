Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

ListingLens ("we", "our", "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how ListingLens handles data collected in the app.

1. Information We Collect

ListingLens is designed to analyze real estate listings locally on your device. We do not require accounts, login, or API keys. The app does not collect or transmit your personal information to any server.

  • Data from your screenshots (e.g., property details) is processed entirely on-device.
  • Any notes, ratings, or tags you add are stored locally.
  • We do not access your camera roll beyond what you explicitly select for analysis.
  • We do not collect location, contacts, or any other personal identifiers.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. Data persists across app launches but is deleted if you uninstall the app or use the "Clear All" feature.

3. No Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services. No property information or user notes leave your device.

4. Analytics & Tracking

ListingLens does not include any tracking, telemetry, or analytics frameworks. Your usage patterns are never transmitted off-device.

5. Security

We follow best practices for local data security on iOS. Your screenshots and analysis results remain on your device and are not uploaded or shared.

6. Children's Privacy

ListingLens is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app and will take effect immediately upon update.
