Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Digital Garage: Car Tracker. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe experience while managing your vehicle records.

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

1. Data Collection and Usage

Digital Garage: Car Tracker is designed as a utility tool for vehicle management. We value your privacy and aim to collect the minimum amount of data possible:

  • User-Provided Information: We store the vehicle details you input, such as brand, model, mileage, and maintenance logs. This data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app.
  • Local Storage: Most of your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store your personal vehicle records on our own external servers unless you explicitly use a cloud backup feature (if available).
  • Diagnostics: We may collect anonymous crash logs and performance metrics to help us identify bugs and improve the app's stability. This data is not linked to your identity.

2. No Third-Party Advertising

Digital Garage: Car Tracker does not display advertisements. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs (like Google AdMob), and we do not track your activity across other apps or websites for marketing purposes.

3. Device Permissions

To provide a complete experience, the App may request the following permissions:

  • Photo Library/Camera: To allow you to upload or take photos of your vehicle or maintenance receipts.
  • Notifications: To send you reminders for upcoming maintenance or insurance renewals.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Since most information is stored on your device, its security is also protected by your device's own security features (like FaceID, TouchID, or Passcode).
