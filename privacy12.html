Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 22, 2024

Welcome to RopeSort ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information regarding our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

RopeSort is designed to be a user-friendly puzzle game. We generally do not require you to register an account or provide personal identification information (such as your name or phone number) to play the game.

  • Game Progress: We may save your levels cleared and high scores locally on your device to ensure a continuous gaming experience.
  • Technical Data: We may collect non-identifying technical information such as your device model, operating system version, and basic usage statistics to help us squash bugs and improve performance.

2. Third-Party Services

Our game may use third-party tools for advertising or analytics (such as Google AdMob, Unity Ads, or Firebase). These third parties may collect and use your device advertising ID (IDFA on iOS or AAID on Android) to provide personalized advertisements and analyze game traffic.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any data processed by the app. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Children's Privacy

RopeSort does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately from our records.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
