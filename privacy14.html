👋 Introduction

SubTrack ("we", "our", or "the app") is a subscription tracking app built to help you manage and monitor your recurring expenses. Your privacy is important to us — this policy explains exactly how we handle your data.

By using SubTrack, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

📱 Data We Collect

We collect nothing. SubTrack does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

All data you enter — subscription names, prices, billing dates, and reminders — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's local storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

🔔 Notifications

SubTrack can send you local notifications to remind you before a subscription renews. These notifications are:

  • Generated entirely on-device
  • Never transmitted to any server
  • Fully optional — you can disable them at any time in Settings

We never access the content of your notifications for any purpose.

📷 Photos & Camera

SubTrack may request access to your photo library to let you import a screenshot for automatic subscription detection (OCR). This feature:

  • Is entirely optional
  • Processes images locally on your device only
  • Never uploads images to any server
  • Does not retain images after scanning

🌐 External Links

SubTrack includes links to third-party websites (such as subscription cancellation pages and the App Store subscription manager). When you tap these links, you leave SubTrack and the privacy policy of the destination site applies. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content or privacy practices of those sites.

📊 Analytics & Advertising

SubTrack contains no analytics tools, no crash reporting services, and no advertising SDKs. We do not track how you use the app, and we do not display ads.

👶 Children's Privacy

SubTrack does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no features that collect personal data from any user, regardless of age.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of SubTrack after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
