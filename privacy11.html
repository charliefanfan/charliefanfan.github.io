Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-05-22

This Privacy Policy describes how FitTrack (the "App") handles your personal information. We take your privacy seriously and strive to collect as little data as possible.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Local Storage: Our App is designed to prioritize user privacy. All workout data, including exercises, sets, and calories, are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults or local databases. We do not transmit this personal workout data to any external servers.

Usage Statistics: We do not track your individual identity or behavior. The App operates independently without requiring a user account.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not share your data with third-party services. We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that collect your personal information.

3. Data Retention

Since your data is stored locally on your device, it remains there until you delete the App or clear the App data through system settings.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any personal identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
